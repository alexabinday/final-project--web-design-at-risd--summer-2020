The Team

Pixie Kolesa

Director, Writer, Character Animator, Character Modeler, Rigger

Pixie is a Media Arts Production major at Emerson College. This is her senior BFA Film! As a result, she wears a lot of hats. In addition to writing the script, she animates our Friedlander model, along with any props or minor characters in the scene. She modeled as well as rigged the Friedlander model. Pixie animates At Emerson, Pixie focuses on animation, but on her own time studies graphic design and typography. Elizabeth-Antiqua is the resulting blend of those two passions.

Prop Modeler

Toni is a senior Media Arts Production major at Emerson College. She built the majortiy of the props used in Elizabeth-Antiqua, as well as some props that double as characters! The mesh of everything from an envelope to a full suit of armour was created by Toni.

Elise Miller

Texture Artist

Elise is a graphic designer from the Buffalo area of New York. She recreated and vectorized patterns made by Elizabeth Friedlander, which were used to texture the models.
