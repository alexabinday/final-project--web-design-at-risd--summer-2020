About Me

Hi! I'm Kokoro Nagaya, or Koko. I'm an aspiring artist/designer. I used to live in Taiwan, but after high school, I decided to study abroad in the United States. Other then art, I'm also a keen harpist and cook, where I someday hope to go to culinary school as well. I'm also a super proud mum to a pomeranian, who goes to university with me in the United States as well!

About My Work

In the past, I primarily did paintings, but I did dabble in sculptures here and there. However, even though I preferred to do work that was more fine art oriented, I now prefer to do more design oriented artworks. Currently, I like to do brand design and book design artworks, but I am also open to trying new things such as learning UIUX.

Education

Before studying abroad, I went to high school in Taiwan, at Taipei European School, where I recieved my International Baccalaureate Diploma. I now study in the U.S. for university, at Rhode Island School of Design, for my MFA diploma. In the past, I preferred working with oil paintings, but as I went into university, I started to gain a liking to design work. So I decided to major in graphic design in university.
